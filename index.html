About

My name is Alex, and I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from Rutgers University. I enjoy writing code to solve real problems and find it satisfying to simplify repetitive tasks. I have experience launching an online retail business, trading/mining digital assets, and writing plenty of code. I hope to one day manage a sizeable real estate portfolio.

I specialize in and prefer Python, but I try to do it all. In the past I've created simulations in unity, websites for myself and clients, react-native apps tethered to a database, python scripts to check over homework, and more. A future project of mine is a python-powered real time digital currency price tracker with built-in notifications.
